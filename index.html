Attack on Titan

The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they fight against Titans, giant humanoid creatures that devour humans. As secrets about the Titans and their own world are uncovered, the story evolves into a complex narrative about freedom, war, and morality.

Hunter x Hunter

Gon Freecss aspires to become a Hunter like his father, who left him when he was young. The story follows Gon and his friends as they take the Hunter Examination and embark on adventures in a world full of magical beasts, treacherous terrains, and powerful enemies.
